# About

## 19-18-8-103 (name)

The name might seem enigmatic, but it's actually a pretty simple concept. Each number is the atomic number of a periodic table element. For example, 19 is Potassium with the symbol K, and 19-18-8-103 together gives "KArOlr". The creator's (my) full name is Karol Roczniak.

## Why is been created

First, because I like it in general. But there are more reasons, and the major one is the current state of the internet and apps overall. They're complicated and incoherent in many ways, and they make us angry, sad, and so on — pretty effectively making the overall human condition worse, in my opinion. And I won't even get started on ads. They're not only almost everywhere, they're impossibly stupid, sloppy, and often about gambling. Not a huge fan. So 19-18-8-103 is not trying to steal your time, make you horny, or make you angry. It just gives you some services you may want to use to get things done.

## What can be done there

- **Contracts**: You can post what you need done — e.g. HVAC service in Dallas. Others can create a company profile that, if it matches the demand, lets them apply to get your contact info and move the deal forward. [Read more about contracts](/src/).

- **Sources**: It's all about debunking false info on the web. If you're an expert in vaccinology and read an article claiming that after some vaccine a third hand will grow on your back, and you disagree and can back it up with arguments and sources, you can just add a critique — and others can read it by checking the URL of the article you're criticizing. [Reader more about sources](/src/).
