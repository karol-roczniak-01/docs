# How Donating Works

People on this platform contribute in different ways — writing critiques, flagging misinformation, completing contracts. Donating is a way to recognize someone's work directly, whatever form it takes.

---

## Sending a Donation

Find the username of whoever you'd like to support, then run:

`donate <name>`

You'll be taken to a short checkout form. Currency is set automatically based on your country — if detection fails, it defaults to USD. Fill in the form and confirm your payment.

Once the transaction is complete, you'll be brought straight back to the app.
